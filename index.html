About Euphoria

Euphoria is the annual cultural festival celebrated every year by Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College Of Engineering. Euphoria provides a platform to the students of the college to showcase their talents in various fields. The highlights of the festival are Paridhan (Intercollegiate Dance Competition), We are the World, Fashion Show and Drama .
